    FAQ

    Are lift tickets, rentals, transportation, meals, or accommodation included?

    No. Lesson prices cover instruction and instructor-related costs only.

    Can half-day lesson times be adjusted?

    Half-day lessons are 9:00-12:00 or 13:00-16:00 for 3 hours. Times are fixed and must be followed.

    Can one group take turns during a lesson?

    Yes, but the lesson fee is calculated based on the total number of people in the group.

    Can I request a specific instructor?

    Specific instructor requests depend on availability and must be confirmed manually.

    Which resorts are registered partner resorts?

    Tomamu, Rusutsu, Sapporo Teine, and Sapporo Kokusai are registered partner resorts. Lessons are subject to each resort’s operating rules.

    Can lessons be arranged at other Hokkaido resorts?

    Other resorts may be available upon request, subject to resort rules, instructor availability, and operating conditions. Please contact us before booking.
