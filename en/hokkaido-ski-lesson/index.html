FAQ

Can I ask for help choosing a Hokkaido resort?

Yes. Tell us your dates, group size, skiing level, accommodation area, and whether you prefer ski or snowboard lessons. We can then discuss suitable resort and instructor options.

Are English-speaking instructors available?

Some instructors can teach in English. Language and instructor availability must be confirmed for your dates before booking.

Can I book ski or snowboard lessons?

Private ski and snowboard lessons may be discussed according to instructor availability, resort rules, and the actual operating conditions.

Is Hokkaido suitable for first-time skiers and families?

Many guests use these lesson formats for first-time learning, family trips, and technique improvement. The lesson plan is confirmed according to the group's needs.

How is the price confirmed?

The final price depends on the date, group size, lesson duration, resort, ski or snowboard choice, and instructor arrangement. Please contact us for confirmation.

Can you arrange lessons at every Hokkaido resort?

No fixed availability is promised for every resort. Other resorts or special arrangements are checked individually according to resort rules, instructor availability, transportation, and operating conditions.
