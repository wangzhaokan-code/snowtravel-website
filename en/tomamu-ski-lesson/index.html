FAQ

Is Tomamu suitable for first-time skiers?

Lesson plans at Tomamu can be discussed for first-time skiers, families, and guests who want to improve their ski or snowboard technique.

Can I request an English-speaking ski instructor at Tomamu?

An English-speaking instructor may be available depending on your dates, group, level, and instructor schedule. Please confirm before booking.

Can I book ski and snowboard lessons at Tomamu?

Ski or snowboard lessons can be discussed according to instructor availability, resort rules, and actual operating conditions.

Is Tomamu suitable for families?

Family and private lesson arrangements can be discussed based on the ages, experience, group size, and instructor availability.

How is the Tomamu lesson price confirmed?

The final price depends on the date, group size, lesson duration, ski or snowboard choice, resort, and instructor arrangement. Please contact us for confirmation.

Can lessons be arranged at other Hokkaido resorts?

Other resorts or special arrangements are checked individually according to resort rules, instructor availability, transportation, and actual operating conditions.
